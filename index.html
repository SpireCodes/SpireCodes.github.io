About Me

Hi! I'm Spire, I work as an engineer at a consultancy and I build keyboards on stream on my Twitch channel here: Twitchspire_blues

My commissions are mostly focussed towards people with little knowledge about keyboards, so I'm used to guiding them towards their ideal build. I keep budget under control by showing people their options when it comes to price vs quality.

That said, I can also work with more complicated builds and I'm happy to have conversations with knowledgeable people who can provide a challenge!
